About Us

Walking in the Ancient Hebrew Path

House of Israel Assembly is a vibrant Hebrew community dedicated to rediscovering the roots of our faith through the original Hebrew lens. We believe in the complete Word of God — unfiltered, unadulterated, and deeply rooted in the ancient traditions of Israel.

Under the anointed leadership of Rabbi Dr. Phillip Banda, we journey together through the Torah, the Prophets, and the Writings, uncovering profound truths that transform lives.

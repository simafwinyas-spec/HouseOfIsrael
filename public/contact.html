FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hebrew Community / Hebrew Roots assembly?

A Hebrew Roots assembly is a congregation of believers who study the Bible through its original Hebrew cultural and linguistic context. We observe Biblical feasts, the Shabbat, and seek to understand the full counsel of God as originally given.

Is this only for Jewish people?

Absolutely not! The God of Israel calls people of every nation, tribe, and tongue. We welcome all who are hungry for the pure Word of God, regardless of background, ethnicity, or denomination. All are welcome at House of Israel Assembly.

Do I need to know Hebrew to join?

Not at all. Beginners are warmly welcomed. Rabbi Dr. Banda teaches in a way that is accessible to all levels. We also offer Hebrew language classes for those who wish to learn.

How can I get involved in the community?

Simply come to any of our services! Shabbat is held every Saturday at 9AM. You can also join our WhatsApp community group, volunteer for events, or enroll in our Torah study classes.
